class GaussianMixture(factors, cancel_method=0)[source]¶

Bases: veroku.factors._factor.Factor

A Class for instantiating and performing operations on multivariate Gaussian mixture functions.

A GaussianMixture constructor.

Parameters

  • factors (Gaussian list) – a list of Gaussian type objects with the same dimensionality.

  • cancel_method (int) –

    The method of performing approximate division. This is only applicable if the factor is a Gaussian mixture with more than one component. 0: moment match denominator to single Gaussian and divide 1: approximate modes of quotient as Gaussians 2: Use complex moment matching on inverse mixture sets (s_im) and approximate the inverse

    of each s_im as a Gaussian
